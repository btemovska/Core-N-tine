About Us

As gyms across US have closed due to COVID-19 and continue to keep their doors shut, Core-N-Tine saw an opportunity and put the idea to work. Our mission is to guide you as frequent gym customer by simply taking full advantage of the items in your own home. Do you have chair at home? Probably. I'm most certain you have an empty room, correct? Take a look at the exercises that a pillow can provide. And for you young beginners, we did not forget you. Simply navigate on the menu at the top page, choose 'Exercise Plan', pick your level and we will provide you 5 days plan of exercises to follow.
